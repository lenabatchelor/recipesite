Black Forest Gateau

A Black Forest Gateau (or Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte in German) is a classic, indulgent chocolate layer cake filled with whipped cream, cherry filling, and a hint of cherry liqueur, all topped with chocolate shavings and more cherries.

Inredients

For the chocolate sponge:

  • 175g plain flour
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 150ml sunflower oil
  • 150ml buttermilk (or milk + 1 tsp vinegar)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the cherry filling

  • 350g pitted black cherries (fresh or tinned)
  • 3 tbsp kirsch (cherry liqueur) or cherry juice
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour

For the whipped cream frosting

  • 500ml double cream (heavy cream)
  • 2 tbsp icing sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the decoration

  • Dark chocolate shavings (about 50g)
  • Extra cherries (fresh or glazed)

Directions

  1. To make the spounge

    • Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and line two 20cm round cake tins.
    • In a bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and bicarbonate of soda.
    • In another bowl, whisk sugar, eggs, oil, buttermilk, and vanilla until smooth.
    • Combine wet and dry ingredients, mix until just combined.
    • Divide the batter between tins and bake for 25–30 minutes, or until a skewer comes out clean.
    • Cool completely on wire racks.

  2. To make the cherry filling

    • In a saucepan, mix cherries, sugar, and kirsch (or juice).
    • Dissolve cornflour in 1 tbsp of water and stir in.
    • Heat until thickened and glossy. Cool before using.

  3. To whip the cream

    • Beat double cream, icing sugar, and vanilla until it forms soft peaks.

  4. Assembel the gateau

    • Slice each sponge horizontally to make four layers.
    • Place one layer on a serving plate, brush with a little kirsch or cherry juice.
    • Spread cherry filling, then a layer of whipped cream.
    • Repeat for remaining layers, finishing with whipped cream on top and sides.
    • Decorate with chocolate shavings and cherries.
  5. Chill

    • Decorate with chocolate shavings and cherries and you're done! :)
